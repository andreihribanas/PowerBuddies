We don’t just want to save money and time for customers but we want our customers to be fully satisfied, as we provide troubleshooting support free of charge for one year. We take pride in providing the best customer service for our customers whom should feel the proficiency of majestic service from our efforts to communicate with consumers.


Our target audience is focused towards those who are on the go and dependent on their phones to get them through the day. Students would benefit from this because it’s affordable, portable and it does not require electrical external sources to recharge the phones during their busy schedules at university. In addition, the time saved by not relying on power sockets to recharge devices, allows business people to charge their phones whilst attending to their everyday business.
