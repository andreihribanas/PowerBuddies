D'Lux PowerMat


Our solar panelled power mat harnesses the most common and eco-friendly form of energy which can be used on the go, the solar energy. We offer our customers the possibility to stay connected for longer in order to enjoy doing what they love the most when using their mobile devices, to either communicate with family/friends, for business purpose or just for general browsing/social networking and accessing apps.

The main advantages of this product are being reflected in the fact that it is very easy to use, it is portable and the wiring required is minimal, henceforth making it consumer friendly.

Another golden feature is that the solar energy benefits the consumer by not having to pay any additional costs to gain power, therefore it is one of the main reasons why people consider it as a viable option.


    Other key features are:


  • Uses shadow tolerant, low light performance which collects energy even from using indirect sunlight by use of bypass diodes which allow for more efficient energy production even on overcast/dull days;

  • Easy to use, light and portable;

  • Freedom of anxiety from running out of power;

  • Zero-carbon emission and environment-friendly;

  • Built-in rechargeable 1350mAh polymer lithium battery.
