Your Web Your Way


You can adapt and customise any website so that it better meets your needs. For example you can increase the size of text, change the colours of the design, or even change the fonts to make content easier to use and comprehend.


Report a Problem with the PowerBuddies website.


If you experience a problem trying to use this website please let us know so that we can provide you with a resolution and, potentially, fix the issue for all users.

Learning and Disability Support Enquiries


If you feel the site doesn't meet the the requirements for people with disabilities then we a Learning Support team that delivers four specialist services, Disability Services, Dyslexia Support, Mental Health and Wellbeing to ensure that our users can functions within using the PowerBuddies site.


Accessibility Conformance


The PowerBuddies Team have worked with accessibility experts to ensure that the University website conforms to guidelines like WCAG 2.0. These guidelines help to explain how to make websites as inclusive as possible.


The PowerBuddies website is monitored continuously and was last formally audited and tested with diverse users in November 2013.
