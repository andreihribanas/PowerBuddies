Senior Assistant Engineer


This role will report to the Renewable Energy Projects Engineer and will have responsibility for appraising the condition and performance of PowerBuddies exiting products and future solar cells production plants.

The Senior Assistant Engineer will manage the supplier/ partner selection process and develop the optimum commercial solution for promoted renewable energy projects.

You will assist with the commissioning of new plants and equipment and oversee the development of large solar farms on various worldwide sites.

You will manage consultant, supplier and contractor resources as required and drive all stakeholders in the delivery of the projects to meet programme, project deliverables and budget.

You will have knowledge of solar powered systems and technologies and their application in a commercial environment with experience in commissioning new processes and evaluating their performance.

You will have managed contractors and consultants and also have a track record of delivering team and personal objectives along with identifying opportunities leading to successful projects.
